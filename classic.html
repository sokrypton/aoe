❓ How to Play

🏆 Goal

Destroy the enemy Town Center 🏰 before they destroy yours! Your Town Center trains villagers and stores your resources — if it falls, you lose.

🧑‍🌾 Gather Resources

🌲 Trees → Wood (build almost everything)
🐑 Sheep & 🍒 berries & 🌾 farms → Food (train units)
⛏️ Gold mines → Gold (soldiers)
🪨 Stone → Stone (walls & towers)

Select a villager, then click a resource. Full hands? They walk it home automatically. Build a Lumber Camp 🪓 / Mill 🛞 / Mining Camp ⛏️ next to resources so the walk is short.

🏗️ Build

🏠 House — +5 population. Build these or you can't train more units!
⚔️ Barracks — trains your army
🧱 Walls & 🗼 Towers — keep raiders out (costs stone)

Select a villager → tap Build Economic or Build Military → pick a building → click the ground.

🛡️ Army & Counters

🔱 Spearman beats 🏇 Scout (big bonus damage)
🏇 Scout beats 🏹 Archer (armor shrugs off arrows)
🏹 Archer beats 🔱 Spearman (and all slow infantry)
🛡️ Militia — solid all-rounder, good vs buildings

Mix your army! One unit type alone gets countered.

🔔 Defend Yourself

Attacked? Ring the Town Bell 🔔 (bottom right) — villagers hide inside the Town Center and towers, which then shoot extra arrows. Ring again to send them back to work. Watch the minimap: anything of yours getting hit blinks white.
